# Blinkit Customer Behavior Analysis Dashboard

## Overview

This project analyzes customer behavior, purchasing patterns, and delivery performance using Blinkit transactional data. The objective is to transform raw business data into actionable insights through SQL analysis and an interactive Power BI dashboard.

The dashboard helps stakeholders understand customer segments, product category performance, customer sentiment, payment preferences, delivery efficiency, and revenue trends.

---

## Dataset

The project uses multiple datasets representing different business functions:

* Customers
* Orders
* Order Items
* Products
* Customer Feedback
* Delivery Performance
* Inventory
* Marketing Performance

The datasets were imported into PostgreSQL and connected using appropriate relationships to create a unified analytical model.

## Results & Insights

### Customer Insights

* Customer segments are fairly balanced across the customer base.
* Positive customer sentiment contributes significantly to overall ratings.

### Sales Insights

* Dairy & Breakfast emerged as the highest-selling category.
* Multiple product categories contribute consistently to overall sales performance.

### Delivery Insights

* Most orders are delivered on time.
* Delayed deliveries represent a smaller portion of total orders and can be monitored for operational improvements.

### Revenue Insights

* Revenue remained relatively stable throughout the selected period.
* Seasonal fluctuations can be identified through the revenue trend analysis.
